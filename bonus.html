Binghamton Bearcat

Bearcat also known as Binturong, is a viverrid native to South and Southeast Asia. It is black in color and represents Basketball

  1. The Binghamton Bearcats are the NCAA Division I athletics teams at Binghamton, New York. United States
  2. A happy bearcat may chuckle, but an irritated one will utter a high-pitched wail or growl fiercely.
  3. They are excellent climbers, moving slowly and cautiously through tree branches, aided by strong feet with semi-retractable claws.
