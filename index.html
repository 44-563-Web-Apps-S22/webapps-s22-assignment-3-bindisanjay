In-Detail description

The closest airport to the location is Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, which is 32 kms.

  1. Follow Airport Approach Road to NH 44 in Madhura Nagar
  2. Continue on NH 44. Take PV Narasimha Rao Expy to Inner Ring Rd/Masab Tank Road/Mehdipatnam - Banjara Hills Rd/Utkoor - Mogdumpur Rd in Ex Service Men Colony
  3. Take Masab Tank Flyover to NH65 in Lakdikapul
  4. Take Lakdikapul Rd to LB Stadium Rd in Ambedkar Colony
  5. Continue on LB Stadium Rd. Take Hyderguda-Basheerbagh Rd and Old MLA Quarters Rd to Himayat Nagar Rd in AP State Housing Board
